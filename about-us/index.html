About Us

Hello neighbors and friends! We're delighted you found us. Since 2021, our family has been growing strawberries to share with you. They are very sweet and "oh so good"! The variety we grow is "spring bearing" only, so we only have strawberries available end of April through May. Stop by, and take some home with you. We look forward to sharing fresh, Home Grown Goodness!
