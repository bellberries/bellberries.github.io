Hours - About us

Our small roadside stand is open Monday-Saturday(closed on Sunday) 9am - 4pm or until sold out of that morning's picking. We pick from our strawberry patch every morning, and sell the strawberries the day we pick them. They are grown on-site, picked on-site, and sold on-site. The freshest you can get! Somedays we sell out by 12 or 1pm.
